About Us

We know you love books. We know you love movies. And nothing beats that exictment when finally! Your favorite characters are making their way to the big-screen like you knew they always deserved.

Equal in the exhilaration... is the dread. Its no secret screen writers and directors take liberties with source materials, our precious authors be damned. Our favorite heroes aren't anything like their book counterpart, important story arcs are cut, completely new elements injected into the existing lore...

That's where Cabbage comes in. Want to know if a book based movie is worth the watch? We give you the cabbage score, a rating scale based on the opinions of the book and movie fans. Find some of your favorite movies and see how many cabbages they score! Want to help us add to our movie shelf? Feel free to contribute!
